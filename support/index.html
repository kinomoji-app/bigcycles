Common questions

Purchases: Kinomoji offers an optional one-time Pro unlock and consumable coin packs, processed by Apple. To restore the Pro unlock on a new device, use Restore Purchases in the app.

Progress: Your game progress is stored only on your device. Deleting the app removes all local progress.
