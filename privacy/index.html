🎬 Kinomoji — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Kinomoji ("the App", "we") is an offline emoji movie-guessing game. This policy explains what data is involved when you use the App. The App works fully offline, requires no account, and does not collect any personal data.

1. Data we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Your game progress (coins, solved puzzles, streaks, stars, settings, language) is stored only on your device using Apple's on-device storage. We operate no servers and never see your data.

2. No ads, no tracking

Kinomoji shows no advertising and does no tracking. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising identifier (IDFA), and no third-party tracking of any kind. The App does not ask for App Tracking Transparency permission because it never tracks you.

3. Purchases (Apple)

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store: a one-time "Pro" unlock and consumable coin packs. There are no subscriptions. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Children

Kinomoji is rated for ages 9+. Because the App collects no personal data and shows no ads, it does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

5. Your choices

Deleting the App removes all locally stored progress from your device. Since nothing is collected or sent anywhere, there is no other data to manage.

6. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.

7. Contact

Questions? Email bigcycles.app@gmail.com.

Kinomoji is a standalone game and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any film studio, distributor, or rights holder. Movie titles are used solely to identify the works being guessed.
